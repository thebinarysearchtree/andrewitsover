Getting Started

Begin by creating an author and then adding some audio or video files to that author.

You can untick "transcribe" if you just want to add the files without any transcription.

Once that is done, you can go back to the Library and see the files that have been added.

Clicking on one of the listings will take you to the details section.

Here, you can see the transcript in full if the media has been transcribed.

Clicking on the markers will start play at that point in the audio or video.

Clicking on the highlighted marker will pause, and clicking another marker will play from that point.
