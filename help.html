Getting Started

Begin by creating an author and then adding some audio or video files to that author. You can untick "transcribe" if you just want to add the files without any transcription. Once you have imported the files, you can click on the author's picture to see them in the library.

Clicking on one of the pictures will take you to the details section for that video. This is where you can see the transcript in full, assuming the media has been transcribed.

Clicking on the markers will start play at that point in the audio or video. Clicking on the highlighted marker will pause, and clicking another marker will play from that point.
